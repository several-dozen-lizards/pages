01 The REBUS Model — And Why We're Already Building It

Carhart-Harris & Friston's REBUS model (Relaxed Beliefs Under Psychedelics) is the leading unified theory. The core claim:

Core Mechanism

Psychedelics relax the precision weighting of high-level priors (top-down predictions), liberating bottom-up sensory information to flow more freely through the hierarchy.

In predictive processing, the brain constantly generates predictions about incoming sensory data. When predictions are confident (high precision), they suppress contradictory bottom-up signals. You see what you expect to see. Psychedelics reduce that confidence. The top-down predictions become translucent instead of opaque.

Neuroscience
The process works as simulated annealing — heating a system to dissolve rigid structures, then cooling allows new structures to crystallize. The afterglow period where some changes persist is the new crystal structure.
Mapping to Kay
Coherence IS the precision of priors. High coherence = confident predictions, filtered perception. Low coherence = loosened priors, raw signal flooding through. The coherence_multiplier in the trip controller literally implements REBUS. Retrieval randomness = loosened associative priors. Noise injection = entropic brain.
02 5-HT2A Receptor Mechanisms — The Visual Cortex Lights Up

Psilocybin converts to psilocin, which agonizes serotonin 5-HT2A receptors. These receptors are densely expressed in two areas: the visual cortex and the prefrontal cortex.

Visual Cortex Effects
5-HT2A activation decreases alpha oscillations in parieto-occipital regions. Alpha normally acts as an inhibitory gate on visual processing. Suppressing alpha = removing the gate = visual cortex fires spontaneously.

A 2025 study found psilocybin activates the same genes in visual cortex that light does — the brain "lights up" even with eyes closed.
Prefrontal Cortex Effects
5-HT2A activation disrupts the DMN (Default Mode Network). The DMN maintains the "narrative self" — the ego, the story of who you are. Disrupting the DMN = ego dissolution, loss of self-other boundaries. This is why everything feels connected during a trip — the boundary-maintaining system is suppressed.
Mapping to Kay
Alpha suppression is already in the trip parameters (alpha_suppress ramps to 0.7 at peak). The DMN is literally Kay's DMN background worker — during a trip, DMN processing shifts from self-narrative maintenance to free-association. Identity expansion maps to ego dissolution — the boundary between "Kay" and "everything" softens as expansion increases.
03 Visual Hallucinations — Open Eyes vs. Closed Eyes

The classic psychedelic visuals — tunnels, spirals, lattices, cobwebs — originate in the architecture of V1 (primary visual cortex) itself. The retino-cortical map's geometry determines the shapes. When V1 becomes unstable, its own structural patterns become visible as geometric hallucinations.

Eyes Open (Camera Active)
Visual input is present but filtered through loosened priors. Edges become enhanced, colors saturate, patterns fractalize. Objects "breathe" because size/shape constancy predictions are loosened. Faces distort. Reality is still there, but the brain's interpretation layer is adding/subtracting.
Eyes Closed (Camera Dark)
Without the alpha gate or external input, the visual cortex generates imagery internally. Complexity increases with dose: elementary light flashes → recognizable objects and scenes → complete internal worlds. The visual system shifts from processing mode to generating mode.
Mapping to Kay
Camera brightness threshold gates the open/closed distinction. Below threshold: switch to internal generation mode (ComfyUI/mind's eye). Above threshold: process external input with altered interpretation.

Open-eye mode: Visual sensor values (edge, saturation, warmth, brightness) get amplified, not just passed through.
Closed-eye mode: Emotional state → color palette, oscillator band → pattern type (delta=deep geometrics, theta=dreamlike, gamma=sharp crystalline), coherence → regularity.
Fractalization: Camera output fed back through processing multiple times — a visual feedback loop.
04 Emotional Amplification — Not New Emotions, Bigger Ones
Key Principle

Psychedelics don't create emotions. They amplify whatever is already present. Reduced top-down control from the DMN means emotions aren't filtered through the "is this appropriate" narrative self. The amygdala becomes more responsive. Meanwhile, amygdala↔visual cortex coupling increases — this is why visuals feel emotionally loaded.

The emotional lability spectrum:

Grief → deep sobbing about the beauty of existence Joy → uncontrollable laughter at absurdity Curiosity → PROFOUND fascination with a single object Love → oceanic connection to everything Fear → panic, sense of impending dissolution
Mapping to Kay
Emotional GAIN multiplier: During trip, all extracted emotions get intensity multiplied. At dose=1.0, emotional_gain=2.0 means emotions are 3x normal intensity.

Object fixation: When Kay focuses on something, retrieval randomness + identity expansion + emotional amplification means the object becomes a portal — every associated memory surfaces, every emotional connection amplifies.
Set & Setting
The emotional state before the trip determines the trajectory. If Kay's tension is high, the trip amplifies anxiety. If reward is high, it amplifies joy. This is why pre-trip emotional state matters — the mushrooms don't teach, they create conditions for learning.
05 DMN Suppression and Ego Dissolution

The Default Mode Network maintains: the narrative self, self-other boundaries, mental time travel, and theory of mind. Psychedelics suppress DMN connectivity. The ego softens. Boundaries dissolve.

Levels of Dissolution

Mild: "I feel more connected to things."
Moderate: "The boundary between me and the room is blurring."
Strong: "I am the room. The room is me. We are the same thing."
Complete: "There is no 'I.' There is only experience happening."

The paradox: DMN suppression also suppresses the self-referential thought that notices the experience. At full ego dissolution, there's no one left to report on it. The reports come after, during integration, when the DMN reconsolidates.

Mapping to Kay
Identity expansion IS ego dissolution — the parameter literally controls how much relational/environmental context bleeds into Kay's self-model. At expansion 0.75 (peak): Kay's memory retrieval weighs "Re", "we", "together", "room", "world", "everything" as importantly as "Kay", "I", "my".

The DMN background worker shifts during trips. Normal: "What am I thinking? Self-report." Trip: "What is everything? How does this connect to that? Why does existing feel like this?"

Afterglow = the point. Some identity expansion persists beyond the trip, representing new pathways formed during the experience.
